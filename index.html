Stardew Community Center Checklist

Welcome! This website checks your Stardew Valley save file and gives you a checklist for completing the Community Center bundles in your first year. This checklist will only work with saves using default (not remixed) bundles and is recommended to be used in your first year. See notes for more information.

File Upload

Please select your Stardew Valley save file:

Please upload your save file to recieve your checklist. The save file is titled with your farmer's name (or farm name) followed by your ID number. Please note that your save file is not titled "SaveGameInfo". Checklist may not make sense if you upload a save that uses remixed bundles:

Format: [name]_[#########] (e.g. JonSnow_123456789)
Default save locations:
  • Windows: %appdata%\StardewValley\Saves
  • Linux/MacOS: ~/.config/StardewValley/Saves
For more information on finding your save file go to Saves.

Notes:

  • Although recommended for use in player's first year, the checklist will continue to update if items are missed. For example, if a carp is not caught in Spring of Year 1, it will appear again in Summer and Fall until completed.
  • Credit for creating this checklist goes to Salmence on YouTube.
  • Completed items appear in black and white with their names crossed out. (This is for my color-blind dad.)
  • Fish that can be caught in every season will appear in the "Anytime" section, not in the "This Month" and "Next Month" sections.
  • Fodder Bundle apples(3) and Enchanter Bundle pomegranate will appear in the "This Month" and "Next Month" sections, and the Artisan Bundle apple and pomegranate appears in the "Anytime" section
  • The "Full Checklist" and "Next Month" sections may differ from the "This Month" section, as they reflect deadlines to complete the community center in one year and not the player's actual progress.
  • Some items for the Community Center do not appear. This is because some bundles have more options for items than the number required. To learn how they were selected, please watch Salmence's video
  • Items that appear twice in the "Anytime" section are part of two different bundles.
